“Live for the line” was a mantra my late Uncle Mike often used. He was a gifted communicator, always making the important point clear and actionable.

He would explain that the brevity of this life is like a mere dot on a line representing eternity. Therefore, we should live for the line. Life is about living for what is eternally valuable: the glory of God in Jesus Christ (John 17:3).

May these short entries contribute to my own journey of “living for the line.”
